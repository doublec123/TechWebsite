About AMTECH

Leading technology retailer in Algeria, delivering premium products and exceptional service across all 58 wilayas

Our Story

AMTECH was founded in 2020 with a vision to make premium technology accessible to all Algerians. What started as a small local shop in Algiers has grown into one of Algeria's most trusted technology retailers, serving customers across all 58 wilayas.

We believe that technology should enhance lives and connect people. That's why we carefully select only the highest quality products from world-renowned brands like Apple, Samsung, and other leading manufacturers. Our commitment to quality, customer service, and nationwide delivery has made us the preferred choice for technology enthusiasts and professionals alike.
